Our Team

Ngoc Lam Pham

Founder & CEO

"I want to bring out the happy 8-year-old me in everyone."

"Hello, I'm Lam, and my name actually means forest. Born and raised in Vietnam for 12 years, I cherish memories of playing soccer with my father at the local park.

Moving to America brought significant changes, and during the Covid pandemic, I faced one of the lowest points in my life. However, our family adapted and settled into a new home.

One day, while exploring my new neighborhood, I stumbled upon a park that instantly rejuvenated me. Realizing the magic of this serene place, I felt compelled to share this experience with others. Thus, I created REKIT, an organization dedicated to bringing people together through shared activities and fostering a sense of community. With REKIT, I aim to make a positive impact, creating lasting memories and connections in the lives of individuals."
